Re-running a job

This procedure describes how to re-run a job in Control-M Self Service.

To rerun a job:

  1. Right-click the service that contains the jobs you want to re-run and select Browse Jobs.
  2. Select a job that has finished running that you want to re-run.
  3. From the Actions drop-down list, select Rerun.

    The job executes. Control-M Self Service automatically refreshes the jobs at defined intervals.

  4. To view the job details, click one of the tabs in the right pane, as described in Job details.
